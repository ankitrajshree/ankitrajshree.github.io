Biography

I am currently a Final Year M.S. student in Computer Engineering at CIDSE, Arizona State University.

For my masters studies, I have been working in the areas of Natural Language Processing and Computer Vision for social media data using a combination of Deep Learning and Cognitive Reasoning Techniques. I work as a Research Assistant in Cognitive Information Processing Systems (CIPS) lab under the supervision of Dr. Hasan Davulcu (Associate Professor and Co-Director, CASCADE ).

I have completed my Bachelors (B.Tech) in Electrical and Electronics from the Electrical Engineering Department, National Institute of Technology, Hamirpur in 2014 with a concentration in Power Systems Operation and Control under the supervision of Dr. Ram Naresh Sharma. After my bachelors I worked with Aricent Technologies for Hughes Network System as my client as a Software Developer for nearly three years before joining ASU to pursue M.S.
